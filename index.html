who are we

About

Shellmates club, is a group of highly motivated university students that are passionate about information security in general. Its diversity of members who are from different Wilayas & different universities is what makes it a special one. We strive to learn and develop our skills by working as a team in organized CTFs and creating our own CTFs for others to learn from our best members.

what we organize

Events

Each year, Shellmates organize many information security events.

HackINI

HackINI is the annual computer security event that initiates amateurs and beginners in the field of computer security. The later has two main aspects : Computer Securty workshops and Capture The Flag (CTF) Competition.

BSides Algiers

BSides is an international full 2 days event in which is delivered security conferences, practical workshops and a very interesting Capture The Flag competition. Shellmates organizes the Algerian version, BSides Algiers.

Forum SIT

This event gathers Information Security professionals in Renaissance Hotel in Telemcen, Algeria. Its a platform of exchange and networking on the subject of security strategies for networks, storage and Cloud. With 30 partnerships, presence of 250 DS/DSSI and the elaboration of 25 conferences and workshops, Forum SIT is the lighthouse of Information Security in Algeria.

Hack Till S'hour

In the time of Ramadhan and its spirituality, Shellmates encourages InfoSec geeks' intelligence by proposing CTF challenges from Ftour to s'hour. An opportunity for fun and traditional atmosphere.
